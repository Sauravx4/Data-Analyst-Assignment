<h1>📊 Customer & Sales Analytics Dashboard (Cognos Assignment)</h1>
<h2>📌 Overview</h2>

This project presents a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard built using IBM Cognos Analytics. The goal of this assignment is to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, and marketing effectiveness using interactive visualizations.

The dashboard provides insights into:

Revenue and sales distribution across locations

Customer segmentation and loyalty behavior

Marketing campaign effectiveness

Customer lifetime value (CLV) trends

<h2>🎯 Objectives</h2>

Analyze sales and profit performance

Understand customer distribution and purchasing behavior

Evaluate marketing response by department

Visualize customer lifetime value across cities

Identify trends and patterns for business decision-making

<h2>📂 Dataset Description</h2>

The dataset used in this project includes:

Feature	Description
Revenue	Total income generated
Profit	Net profit from sales
Quantity Sold	Number of units sold
Customer Location	Geographic data
Departments	Product categories
Loyalty Status	Customer segmentation (VIP, Gold, etc.)
City	Location-based sales data
Marketing Coupons	Campaign response tracking
<h2>📊 Dashboard Components</h2>
1. 🌍 Revenue & Quantity by Location

A map visualization showing:

Revenue distribution geographically

Quantity sold across regions

Helps identify high-performing markets

2. 🎯 Marketing Response by Department

Circular visualizations representing:

Coupon response rates (Coupon 1–6)

Departments like:

Computers & Home

Kitchen Appliances

Photography

Electronics

Useful for evaluating campaign effectiveness

3. 🧩 Department Sales by Loyalty Status

Bubble chart showing:

Sales distribution by customer loyalty tiers:

Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, VIP

Highlights:

Which customer segment contributes most to revenue

Department-wise engagement

4. 📈 Customer Lifetime Value & Quantity Sold

Combined bar + line chart:

Bars → Quantity Sold

Line → Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Cities included:

London, Toronto, Los Angeles, Vancouver, etc.

Helps analyze:

Long-term customer value vs short-term sales
